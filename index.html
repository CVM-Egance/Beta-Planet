Explore the unknown planet " Beta planet " with your spaceship , avoid obstacles and enemies and collect energy points to avoid dying , Good luck commander.

Controls

A/◄ and D/► to strafe · SPACE to Boost · P to Pause · Mouse / touch drag works.

Energy drains much faster now! Collect orbs to refill. Watch for rare moving obstacles. Colliding ends the run.

Every 500 points, the background color changes. Survive longer to increase score and speed.

New: Collect blue shields for temporary invincibility!

Power-ups: Collect blue bullets to shoot obstacles for 7 seconds!

Gravity Zones: Beware of areas that will pull your ship!
